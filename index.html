ABOUT

Hatim Benhsain is a game designer, comic artist, 2D animator, programmer, writer, musician, and composer currently living between Rabat and Abu Dhabi. He holds a Bachelor of Arts from NYUAD where he studied Interactive Media and Music and Sound Computing, and he has studied Game Design at the NYU Game Center and Japanese Gaming Culture at Waseda University in Tokyo.

His work tends to deal with queerness, depression, and alienation and is often satirical, fantastical, or autobiographical. He uses his art to express his anxieties about living under capitalism, religionâ€™s control over society and culture, and the current direction of the tech and art world. Most recently, he was a resident at the Brainmind residency for which he developed an interactive fiction about his relationship with his dad. He also used to play the keyboard in a band he co-founded, 1 AM.
